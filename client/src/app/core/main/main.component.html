The Besties' Book Club is an online community that aims to fill in your cup of motivation and inspiration to become a more well-read and educated lady while making friends discussing the most amazing and inspiring books of all time.

Create Your Post

In your post you are free to share your thoughts and notes about the book you are currently reading, an author who has impressed you, a favorite quote from a book or a topic which you want to share and discuss with us!

Review our Books

Each month we have a book of the month and other recommendations from which you can choose your next read. Feel free to like and comment on our recommendations so we can easily decide the book of the next month.
